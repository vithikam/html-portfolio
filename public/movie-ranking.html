The Best Movies According to Vithika

My Top-3 movies of all time.

Pride and Prejudice

This is based on classic novel Pride and Prejudice. I love the novel and the movie takes from the novel nicely.

The Others

This movie is in horror genre but the fear element is rarely on the surface; always forming an undertone in the movie. The twist comes as a realisation that connects the rest of the movie.

Sholay

This Bollywood Movie is a classic that mixes all the elements romance, drama, action, suspense and comedy to deliver a wholesome 2.5 hrs experience.
